Edzag Case Study

Overview:

    The purpose of the Edzag, India is to build a Digital Educational Institute. I was contacted by the board member to help with the website design as a part of the overall rebranding of the organisation.

Problem

    Edzag wants to develop a web app that will help students, teachers and parents to track educational academic programs. Apart from these general features, they also want a platform which helps the institute to build and showcase digital Institute, brand building, increase revenue with the support of Edzag Studio program and Asset monetization, receive massive educational info and delivery platform through Edzag Exchange.

Research Plan

    Research enables me to dig deep into my understanding of users - not only their immediate frustrations, but also their hopes, fears, abilities, limitations, reasoning, and goals. It lays essential foundations for creating solutions in later stages.

    To ensure the research stays on track and better guide the responsive website design later, it is important to create a research plan before diving into the research phase. I listed research goals, research questions, assumptions, methodologies, participants, and timeline in my research plan.
